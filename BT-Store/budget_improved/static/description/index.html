Budget improved

Extends the analytic accounts with new smart buttons.

Smart Sales button

Total amount of sales from all sales orders of an analytic account which are in state sale or done.

Smart Invoice button

Total amount of the invoice lines of an analytic account whose invoices are in state open or paid.

Other features

Calculating the Practical amount of the budget line when the analytic account is not set through account move lines.
